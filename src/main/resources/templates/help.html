Help Page - RSS25SB Operations

  • GET /: Displays the home page (HTML).
  • GET /help: Displays this help page (HTML).
  • GET /rss25SB/resume/xml: Lists all articles in a summarized format (XML).
  • GET /rss25SB/resume/html: Lists all articles in a summarized format (HTML).
  • GET /rss25SB/resume/xml/{id}: Retrieves detailed information about an article by its GUID (XML).
  • GET /rss25SB/html/{id}: Retrieves detailed information about an article by its GUID (HTML).
  • POST /rss25SB/insert: Adds a new article to the feed (XML input, XML output).
  • DELETE /rss25SB/delete/{id}: Deletes an article by its GUID (XML output).
